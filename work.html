What we do

We are focused on enabling the long-term success of the technologies aligned with its purpose.

In 2025, the Foundation will further adapt the scope of its responsibilities to the current needs of the Radworks Ecosystem, its grantor. In its role as protector of the Ecosystem, the Foundation will steward identification and responses to Ecosystem-wide needs and risks in an increasingly evolving regulatory environment. Its 2025 Org proposal and budget can be found here.

The Foundation empowers Radworks products and contributors by identifying and clearing potential, future operational challenges.

If you are an individual, initiative, or organization with a mission or goals aligned with those of the Better Internet Foundation, please contact us. We are always looking for like-minded partners to realize our purpose.
